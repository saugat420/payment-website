What if I am a complete beginner?

Perfect. Course 1 starts from absolute zero and builds your foundation step by step.

No. AI tools remove the need for coding in most marketing work.

What if I miss a live session?

All Monday and Thursday sessions are recorded. Live attendance is strongly recommended, but you can watch later.

How much time should I commit?

Plan for 5 to 10 hours per week, including live sessions, self-study, and assignments.

What if I cannot complete everything in 90 days?

You have lifetime access and can rejoin any future batch for free.

Will I get clients during the program?

Many students do, especially with the client acquisition bonuses and Freedom Achiever Challenge. Results depend on your effort, market, and execution.

You need a laptop and internet connection. Many AI tools have free tiers. Paid tools like ChatGPT Plus or Meta Ads can help, but the program will guide you on what is worth using.

Is this only for Nepali students?

No. The program is taught in a Nepali-English mix with Nepal-focused examples, but AI marketing is a global skill.
